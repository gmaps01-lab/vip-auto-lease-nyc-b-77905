Frequently Asked Questions About Luxury Car Leasing

How does the $0 down luxury car leasing process work at VIP Auto Lease NYC?

Once approved, we can deliver your vehicle anywhere in NYC, often within 48 hours. All you need are valid NYS driver’s license, proof of insurance, and qualifying credit.
Are there any additional fees beyond the advertised monthly payment?
While our $0 down offers cover the down payment, there may still be standard fees required in New York State, such as tax, title, acquisition, and DMV registration fees. We pride ourselves on transparency, so we’ll provide a full breakdown of costs before you sign. There are no hidden fees, and all charges will be clearly listed in your lease agreement.
Can I lease a luxury car if I have out-of-state or international driver’s license?
Yes, we work with many clients who are new to NYC or on temporary assignments. If you have a valid out-of-state or international license, we can assist you with your lease. However, New York State law may require you to obtain a local license within a certain period. Our team will walk you through the necessary steps and documentation to ensure compliance with state regulations.
How long does it typically take to get approved and receive my leased vehicle?
Most approvals are completed within a few hours, provided all required documents are submitted. Once approved, we usually deliver vehicles within 24-48 hours to customers in Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, or Staten Island. If the model you want isn’t immediately available, we’ll give you a realistic timeline, usually within 3-5 business days for special orders.
What happens if I want to end my lease early or have issues with my vehicle?
If you need to end your lease early, we offer flexible options such as lease transfers or early termination (subject to the terms of your contract). For vehicle issues, all our leased cars are covered under the manufacturer’s warranty. If you experience any problems, simply contact us and we’ll coordinate service with the appropriate dealership in the NYC area.
